I'm Zayed — an IoT Engineer with a Bachelor's degree in Internet of Things from Savonia University of Applied Sciences.

I've built OPC UA systems for critical infrastructure at Savonia's WaterLAB and Lucas-Nülle Smart Factory, developed post-quantum cryptographic tunnels under the AIQUSEC project with Business Finland, and contributed to digital twin models used for real-time monitoring and anomaly detection.

Beyond the technical work, I care about continuous learning and innovation — finding ways to make tomorrow's connected systems not just smarter, but safer.
