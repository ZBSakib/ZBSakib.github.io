I'm Sakib — an IoT Engineer with a Bachelor's degree in Internet of Things from Savonia University of Applied Sciences.

My work sits at the intersection of industrial automation, cybersecurity, and smart infrastructure. I've built OPC UA systems for critical infrastructure at Savonia's WaterLAB, developed post-quantum cryptographic tunnels under the Business Finland funded AIQUSEC project, and built digital twin models — from Ignition HMI dashboards to full 3D Unity environments — with real-time anomaly detection achieving F1 scores near 0.9.

Beyond the technical work, I care about making tomorrow's connected systems not just smarter, but provably safer.
